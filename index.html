About

Amrita School of Arts and Sciences located in the sprawling and serene Brahmasthanam Temple Complex, with rich biodiversity, on the main NH-17, is leaping towards boundless glory and fame. The campus with most modern infrastructure, faculty and administrative set up has excellent landscape abundant with trees and gardens. School offers Ph.D., M.Phil., SIX Post Graduate (P.G.), FOUR Integrated U.G. - P.G. and Five Under Graduate (U.G.) programme under various departments. Amrita School of Arts and Sciences, Kochi functions as a regular college in the Kochi Campus of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham and is approved by UGC and Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India, as per Order no. F.20-47/2005-U.3 dated 19th November 2008. As per statutory norms Int. BCA-MCA course is approved by the AICTE (F.No.AB/AICTE/REG/2018. 1-4618462931). Prior to the formal induction into the ambit of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham in 2005, the institute was affiliated to the Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT). Amrita School of Arts and Sciences provides Ashram hostel for girls and college bus facility for day scholars coming from Kochi city and suburbs.
